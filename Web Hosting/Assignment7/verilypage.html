Verily is Alphabet Inc.'s organization focused on health sciences. Recently, Verily has announced a new partnership with the continuous glucose monitoring (CGM) company known as Dexcom. Verily's goal in working with Dexcom to create a more comfortable, less bulky, and more affordable CGM for diabetics. Typically a continuous glucose monitor (CGM) such as the Dexcom G6 which is the latest model released by Dexcom is used by diabetics to constantly track their glucose levels rather than having to prick their finger multiple times a day. Although this technology is an impressive innovation within the diabetes community, it's an expensive device that many diabetics can't afford which is why Verily's mission to lower the cost of the device is so important to many diabetics.
